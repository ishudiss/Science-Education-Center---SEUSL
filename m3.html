Department of Mathematical Sciences

Mathematical science is the universal language of patterns and structures used to explain the world around us. It involves the study of numbers, shapes, quantities, and patterns, and their interactions through formulas, equations, and algorithms. This discipline serves as the foundation for various fields like physics, engineering, economics, and more. It helps in problem-solving, predicting outcomes, modeling natural phenomena, and advancing technology, making it an essential tool for understanding and shaping our modern world.
