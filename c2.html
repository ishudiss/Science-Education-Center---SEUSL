Department of Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of algorithms, data, and computation. It encompasses the exploration of theoretical foundations and practical applications related to computing. This discipline delves into designing and developing software, creating algorithms, analyzing and storing data, and understanding computer systems and networks. It's a diverse field influencing innovation in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software engineering, and much more. Computer Science shapes the digital world we live in, driving advancements in technology, communication, and problem-solving.
